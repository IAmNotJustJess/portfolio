2019

ByleCos.pl - 1st Edition

Roles: Developer, Gameplay Designer

An another refresher from our RoulyCraft team! This time a more classic Survival with a custom made map and a created from scratch player villages system! Most of the map was in a PvE zone, but inside of Villages and the Nether biomes PvP was enabled.
I configured plugins and created the entire village system, which includes: village levels, village professions, village-wide buffs and more!

2020

ByleCos.pl - 2nd Edition

Roles: Developer, Gameplay Designer

Ah, the second edition of ByleCos! We were really inspired by the XVII'th Century look of Poland, so all additions were based on what you could find during said times! The server had a custom map that recreated Poland's territory of said times, though scaled down a bit.
I created a statistics system; a new rank-up system; a new job system; an injuries and ailments system and an election system. Additionally to give players new tools of combat I also created many custom firearms, bombs and cannons!

2020

Various third generation motion design projects

Roles: Motion Designer

The latest and greatest generation of my motion design projects.
The rate of said projects slowed down during this time but I can gladly say that I finally understood how to make stuff look good and relatively fast.
I made even more animated intros for YouTubers and friends of mine. As well as some small additions for some videos.

2020

ByleCos.pl - 3rd Edition V1

Roles: Developer, Gameplay Designer

An another edition of our beloved ByleCos project! This time we wanted to add more RPG elements to it. Eventually the project didn't come to fruition though. There's no much I can say about other than how I finally coded something really modular and advanced, that being firearms! You could basically change every bit about the weapon, bullet count, ammo, action types, rate of fire, spread and more!
Even though it didn't get to a point of being a thing I worked on a new advanced modular firearm system, RPG statistics and levelling system.

2021

ByleCos.pl - 3rd Edition V2

Roles: Developer, Gameplay Designer

The 2nd version of our third edition. We scrapped the old RPG aspects to touch something that we all agreed was really good - the progression system! We wanted to implement a custom "through the ages" system, where you completed quests to unlock new ages of machinery that could help you with obtaining new cool stuff! Unfortunately this project got shelved for the time being.
I worked on implementing new features for animated menus, custom machinery with their own power sources and utilities, new tools and more.

2022

BedSMP

Roles: Developer

There's not a lot of stuff I could talk about this project, since it was simple from the get-go: make your usual SMP server, with data-packs from the internet and a new twist! That being BedWars-like beds for our YouTubers! Since most if not all of them made content on the BedWars Mini-Game it only felt natural!
I worked on implementing the Bed being a respawn beacon for YouTubers and the upgrade system for them to utalise to get even stronger!

2023

Tidal Playlist Modifier

Made by me :D

A friend of mine had a problem with Tidal, he had some songs in his playlist that weren't in the highest quality possible. So I wrote this small little script that looks for highest quality audio of the songs you have in your playlist based on several criteria. Unfortunately it is not spotless, because of how some tracks info being weird but it does work most of the time! It was my first time doing something like this with Python but it was quite alright to be honest!

Hello! IAmNotJustJess here!

I am a 21 year old developer from Poland, currently on the way to obtain an engineering degree in Computer Science.
I specialise in Software Development and Motion Design. I do also love working with anything that involves computers and all kinds of art!
I started my programming adventures at around 12 years old, thanks to the use of a Minecraft Server-Side Scripting Language called simply Skript. Ever since I worked on many projects in the Minecraft scene, starting with RoulyCraft, eventually ending at the latest BedSMP.
I transitioned from using Skript to Java in 2022, which fortunately went really smoothly as I ported one of the Mini-Games I wrote in Skript to Java!
In 2017 I finally started playing around with Adobe After Effects, which helped me obtain new skills that allowed me to understand how to make something visually appealing.
Nowadays I am messing around with the Godot engine and websites (such as this!) and I hope that this can allow me to hopefully put my skills on the professional level!
